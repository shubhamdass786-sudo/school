Sciences & Technology

STEM Education

At Howard Lane Senior School, our Sciences & Technology curriculum is designed to challenge students and inspire a lifelong passion for learning. With state-of-the-art laboratories and expertly trained faculty, students dive hands-on into physics, chemistry, biology, and computer science.

We equip our students with the critical thinking, problem-solving, and analytical skills necessary to excel in the rapidly evolving digital and scientific landscape of the 21st century.

Mathematics & Business

Analytical Foundations

Our Mathematics and Commerce programs focus on building strong analytical foundations and entrepreneurial mindsets for tomorrow's business leaders and problem solvers.

Students are encouraged to apply mathematical concepts to real-world scenarios, preparing them for higher education and careers in finance, economics, accounting, and business management.
