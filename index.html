Our Mission & Vision

Nurturing Potential, Shaping Futures

Our vision at Howard Lane Senior School is to empower students through a dynamic curriculum that offers ample opportunities for their personal and academic growth. We are committed to nurturing holistic development, helping students discover their innate talents, and instilling in them values that define true humanity.

We equally prioritize excellence in academics, sports, cultural appreciation, and moral values, creating well-rounded individuals who are not only academically proficient but also socially responsible. In addition to this, we recognize the significance of technological advancement and the importance of cultivating leadership skills, self-discipline, and a scientific mindset among our students.

To achieve our mission, we have embraced computer-based education, artificial intelligence, and cultural values, and we instill in our students a deep love for their mother tongue and their home state. Through these endeavors, we aim to prepare our students to excel academically and to be well-rounded, compassionate, and responsible citizens of the world.

Principal's Message

Welcome to Howard Lane

Dear Students, Parents, and Esteemed Members of the Howard Lane Community,

Welcome to Howard Lane Senior School, where education transcends textbooks and classrooms, dedicated to shaping the future by nurturing young minds and fostering overall growth. Our vision is to provide holistic education that empowers students for an ever-evolving world, establishing a robust foundation for promising futures.

In the digital age, we emphasize the role of technology in education. Our school, a technology hub, equips students with digital skills vital in the 21st century. We promote a scientific mindset, nurturing critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity. Beyond academic excellence, we value physical health and character development. Sports instill teamwork, leadership, and well-being. We offer vocational programs, preparing students for diverse careers. Our mission extends to cultivating responsible global citizens, inspiring students to engage with global issues, promote diversity, and practice empathy.

Parents and students, your active participation in our community fosters a sense of belonging and shared commitment. Together, we create an environment where students thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Entrusting your child's education to us is an honor. This year, we're excited to introduce artificial intelligence (AI) into our curriculum, teaching AI to our students. We anticipate a year of growth, learning, and inspiring students to be active, responsible global citizens.

Wishing everyone a successful and enriching academic year ahead.

Warm regards,
Balraj Singh Batth
(Principal)
